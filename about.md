Backend developer at **Amazon**, building and deploying ML and Generative AI models at scale.

Previously at **Indus OS**, built Text-to-Speech systems for 6 Indic languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Bengali, Marathi) on Android NDK in C. Also worked on ML models for user analytics.

**M.S. by Research** from **IIT Madras** in physics-based animation under Prof. Sukhendu Das. DAAD exchange scholar at University of Stuttgart under Prof. Thomas Ertl.
